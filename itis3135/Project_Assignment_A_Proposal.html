Konnichiwa: a Japan Travel Guide


Overview

  • The application created is intended to guide the user through travel basics, including a schedule maker that can create a user's own schedule and the cost for the trip. The website will include a page that explains how to prepare for a trip to Japan, as well as another page explaining why you should take a trip there and the steps on how to plan the trip. The website will also include 5 pages, with each page representing a recommended place to visit, a description of the place, landmarks, places to eat, and activities available in the area.
  • The intended users of the website are those who are looking to plan a trip to Japan in the future and those who are looking for what the country has to offer. The website will attract those who have a passion to travel to different countries and places around the world, or for those who wants to learn a little bit about Japan.
  • When the user loads into the website, they will be welcomed with a homepage that has navigation throught the different webpages. There will be a secion for each page, with the 5 places to visit being located as one secion of the navigation menu.
