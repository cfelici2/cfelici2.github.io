Website Evaluations

Best Buy

Best Buy is an electronic retail store that sells a wide variety of products including phones, TVs, computers, and much more.

  • Contrast: Best Buy's use of the colors black, white, yellow, and blue was used in a way that made the content of their page stand out.
  • Repetition: The repeated use of their colors for their product listing and advertisements made it seem professional for their users.
  • Alignment: The alignment for the design of the website and their listings are very organized and are easy for users to navigate.
  • Proximity: Best Buy grouped different design elements for users to know where to navigate through different categories and listings.

Craigslist

Craigslist is a website which offeres users to post any services, trades, or items to sell on their website. it consist a wide range of listings, from job posts to individual items that are listed for sale.

  • Contrast: Craigslist barely has any use of colors besides white, gray, and blue for their background color and text color.
  • Repetition: The use of blue text with the use of a white or gray background makes the website look unfinished.
  • Alignment: The alignment of many listings in the website are off, which can be difficult for users to navigate.
  • Proximity: Craigslist does not do a good job of grouping design elements and colors to make it easy for users to read through.
